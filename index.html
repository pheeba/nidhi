ABOUT US

Company History

ART Group of companies is a conglomerate of finance related institutions. ART Leasing Ltd. falls under the category of Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. It was founded in 1990 by its Chairman Mr. V.S. Raveendran Pillai. By 1993, the management team was expanded to 10 directors to manage the growing business activities. Our core business is to provide personal and business loans secured by gold jewellery, and Chit Fund Management. We also provide consultancy service for buying and selling of prime properties anywhere in India, especially Kerala. Our ancillary services include Fund Transfer through Western Union. The promoters of the group are a team of highly experienced industry experts with a vision to establish customer-friendly branches in the emerging markets of Kerala.

By 1997, the management decided to expand its wings in strategically potential locations. The ART Head Office is registered at Chengannur, Kerala. The Head Office premise is a spacious multistoried building wholly owned by the group which controls the complete operations of over 74 branches spread across Central Travancore. Currently, it owns land at prime locations in Tiruvalla and Chengannur. The ART group comprises of four professionally managed finance companies.
ART Leasing Limited(N.B.F.C) is the flagship company of the group which provides a bouquet of comprehensive financial services that is rendered with minimal formalities and maximum flexibility. Chengannur Chits Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in 2012-13 and is conducting chits as per Kerala Licence chit. ART Developers and Infrastructures (P) Limited is a consultancy firm providing real estate services for purchase and sale of prime properties.
The management of ART Group of companies understands the need of the customers and has devised the financial packages to suit every individual’s needs. Our customer base constitute people from different walks of life; be it a businessman or a salaried individual, vendor or a farmer, we provide equal opportunity to everyone for availing our financial services and reap its benefits. With minimum time to secure a gold loan, a customer is happy not to be burdened with unnecessary formalities. We are spread across Central Travancore area and gradually expanding to other potential regions with our well-devised strategies for market penetration.
We have dedicated and experienced staff to serve the customers with various schemes that gives us a competitive edge over other financial institutions. Our infrastructure and resources are well-connected to provide a proactive and customer-friendly environment at every branch. Check the branch nearest to you and visit us today.

Mission & Vission

To establish a network of 100 branches all over Kerala in the shortest span of time and render sophisticated technology-driven services to the customers.

The management team of ART group of companies envisions an improved standard of living for the people of Kerala in the near future which would need a stronger purchasing power. This need can be catered with convenient investment packages that suit every individual’s capacity.

  A.R.T LEASING LIMITED


ART LEASING LIMITED falls under the category of Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) regulated by the Reserve Bank of India.ART Leasing Limited started its operations in 1990 as a private limited company and in a short span of 5 years the business operation grew significantly. It was then incorporated as a public limited company in 1995. Realizing the potential for financial services in Central Travancore region, the promoters identified prominent locations in this region and established branches for the convenience of the customers.

ART Leasing Limited is the flagship company of the group. It provides comprehensive financial services to cater the needs of the customers without any hassle. Unlike banks and other financial institutions, we have minimal formalities to extend loans and credit facilities. Personal Loan against Gold Jewellery, Business loans for SME, Instant Fund Transfer through Western Union, MoneyGram and Xpress Money are the financial services that are rendered through our branches. With an established network of financial services, the hospitality service adds value to our brand name.

Over 19 branches across Central Travancore and many more to come in the near future, ART Leasing Limited is a fast growing brand in financial services. Our customers repeatedly avail our services and refer our good name to their dear ones. This indeed is the secret of our growth which strengthens us to venture to new regions.
